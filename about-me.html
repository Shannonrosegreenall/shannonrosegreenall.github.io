My name is Rose Baker and this is my "garden" of work.

Ever since I was little I loved taking photos.  Throughout school I have been lucky enough to
pursue photography as a career.  My work now looks a lot better than it did at age 8!

I am based in Auckland City, New Zealand and specialise in natural light portrait photography
with minimal post processing.  

I am always on the hunt for more models, so if you are interested head over to
my CONNECT page and give me a buzz.

I have a flat rate of $250 a session.
After the shoot I require 3 business days for minor edits.
Once that is complete you will have access to your pick of 20 images from a select 50.

Hope to hear from you soon!
Keep on growing,
Rose.
